About TrustLens AI

Built for anyone who reads reviews before they buy

Fake reviews skew ratings, bury real feedback, and cost buyers money. TrustLens AI gives shoppers, sellers, and researchers a fast, transparent way to check whether a review reflects a real experience — and shows its reasoning instead of just handing back a label.

Process

From paste to verdict in four steps

The same pipeline runs on every scan, in this order.

01

Paste the review

Drop in text from Amazon, Google Maps, Yelp, or any App Store listing.

02

Set the star rating

Match the rating that was left alongside the review — it's checked against the text itself.

03

BERT-Ensemble analyzes it

Linguistic, sentiment, and feature models run in sequence to build a confidence score.

04

Read the explainable verdict

Get the classification plus the heatmap and reasoning behind it — no black box.

Capabilities

What TrustLens checks for you

Under the hood

Built on an explainable ML pipeline

TrustLens combines classic NLP features with an ensemble classifier, then explains its own reasoning.

NLP

TF-IDF & Linguistic Features

Word patterns, punctuation, capitalization, and vocabulary richness are extracted from every review.

Ensemble

BERT-Ensemble Classifier

A scikit-learn / XGBoost ensemble weighs each feature to produce the final authenticity score.

XAI

Explainable AI Layer

Every verdict comes with the specific signals that drove it — sentiment mismatch, promo density, and more.

API

FastAPI Backend

A dedicated prediction service scores reviews in real time and powers the Chrome extension too.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

TrustLens AI analyzes the review text and star rating you paste into the analyzer. That text is sent to our prediction service for the sole purpose of generating a verdict, confidence score, and explanation.

Where your scan history lives

Your past scans are stored locally in your browser's storage, not on our servers. Clearing your browser data or using the "Clear History" button removes it permanently.

What we don't do

We don't sell review text to third parties, use it to train models without disclosure, or share it with advertisers. The Chrome extension only reads review content on supported pages when you have it active.

Your choices

You can clear your local scan history at any time from the sidebar. Since analysis happens per request, simply not submitting a review keeps it out of our systems entirely.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the TrustLens AI team through the project's repository.
